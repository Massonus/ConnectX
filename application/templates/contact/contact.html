Terms & Warranty

At TechPro, we prioritize your satisfaction and stand behind the quality of our repairs. Here's what you need to know.

Service Terms

  • Free Diagnostics: Every repair begins with a complimentary diagnostic assessment to identify the issue accurately.
  • Transparent Pricing: You'll receive a detailed quote before any repair work begins, ensuring clarity and transparency.
  • Quality Parts: We use only high-quality parts in our repairs to ensure optimal performance and longevity.
  • Quick Repairs: Repair times vary depending on the issue. Our technicians strive to complete repairs swiftly, typically within 1-2 days.

Warranty Policy

  • Quality Guarantee: We stand by the quality of our repairs. If you experience any issues related to the repaired component, simply bring your device back within 14 days for a complimentary reevaluation.
  • Parts Warranty: Parts used in our repairs are covered under a 3-month warranty against defects.
  • Exclusions: Our warranty does not cover damages resulting from accidents, misuse, or unauthorized modifications.

Customer Satisfaction

  • Dedicated Support: Our friendly and knowledgeable staff are always available to address any questions or concerns you may have before, during, or after the repair process.
  • Feedback Welcome: We value your feedback and strive to continuously improve our services. Feel free to share your experience with us.

If you have any questions, concerns, or need expert guidance, our dedicated team is just a message or call away. Feel free to reach out for any post-repair support, device-related inquiries, or simply to share your experience. We're committed to delivering not only exceptional repairs but also unparalleled customer service. Your trust in TechPro is valued, and we're here to make your mobile repair journey smooth and stress-free. Contact us today, and let us continue to exceed your expectations!
